About TPS Roofing Solutions

TPS Roofing Solutions is a full-service roofing company for residential and commercial properties across Tampa Bay and greater Central Florida. Our growing service area includes Hillsborough, Pasco, Pinellas, Hernando, Polk, Sumter, Citrus, Marion, and Levy counties.

Roof Installation

New Roofs, Replacements & Material Expertise

Whether you're building new, replacing an aging roof, or upgrading to a higher-performance system, TPS Roofing Solutions handles every type of residential and commercial roof installation. We work with all major roofing materials and build to Florida code.

New Roof Installation

Complete new roof systems for new construction and additions. Engineered for Florida's climate, built to code, and backed by manufacturer warranties.

Roof Replacement

Tear-Off & Re-Roof

Full tear-off and replacement of your existing roof system. We remove old materials, inspect the decking, and install a brand-new roof from the ground up.

Overlay Roofing

Installing a new roof over your existing system when conditions allow. A cost-effective option that saves time and reduces waste.

Asphalt Shingles

Architectural, 3-tab, impact resistant, and cool roof shingles. The most popular residential option — versatile, durable, and cost-effective.

Tile Roofing

Concrete, clay, Spanish, and Mediterranean tile. Beautiful, durable, and built for Florida's architecture. Specialty clay tile work available.

Metal Roofing

Standing seam, corrugated, aluminum, metal shingles, and agricultural metal roofs. Long-lasting and energy efficient.

Flat & Commercial Systems

TPO, PVC, EPDM, modified bitumen, built-up roofing (BUR), torch down, and self-adhered membranes for commercial and industrial properties.

Industrial Roofing

Warehouse, factory, industrial membrane systems, and cold storage roofing. Built for large-scale commercial and industrial facilities.

Custom Roof Design & Installation

Complex roof designs and specialty installations. We work with your architect or engineer to execute the plan with precision.

Roof Repair

Fast Response. Expert Repairs. All Materials.

From a small leak to major storm damage, TPS Roofing Solutions provides expert roof repair services for residential and commercial properties. We diagnose the problem, explain your options clearly, and get it fixed right the first time.

Leak Repair

Pinpoint leak detection and repair for all roof types. We trace the source, fix the problem, and prevent future water intrusion.

Storm Damage Repair

Rapid assessment and repair after hurricanes, tropical storms, and severe weather events. We work with your insurance to get you covered.

Wind Uplift Repair

Repair and resecure roofing materials displaced by high winds. Critical for maintaining structural integrity and preventing further damage.

Structural Roof Repair

Assessment and repair for structural concerns including the 25% rule. We inspect, diagnose, and coordinate with specialists when needed.

Flashing Repair

Repair and replacement of chimney flashing, step flashing, counter flashing, and valley flashing to stop leaks at penetration points.

Shingle & Tile Replacement

Individual and section replacement for damaged shingles, tiles, ridge caps, nail pops, and underlayment. Matched to your existing roof.

Know the True Condition of Your Roof

Real Estate Roof Inspections

Roof Restoration

Extend the Life of Your Existing Roof

Not every roof needs to be replaced. TPS Roofing Solutions offers restoration services that extend the functional life of your roof through rejuvenation treatments, coatings, and targeted restoration work — saving you money and reducing waste.

Roof Rejuvenation Treatments

Bio-based treatments that restore flexibility and waterproofing to aging shingles, adding years of life to your existing roof.

Roof Sealing

Professional sealing to protect against water infiltration, UV damage, and premature wear. Essential for flat and low-slope roofs.

Roof Coating Restoration

Application of high-performance coatings to restore and protect your existing roof surface. Reflective options available for energy savings.

Tile Roof Restoration

Cleaning, re-sealing, and repair of tile roofs to restore their appearance and weather performance without full replacement.

Metal Roof Restoration

Cleaning, coating, and repair of metal roof panels, seams, and fasteners. Extend the life of your metal roof system by decades.

Roof Maintenance

Prevent Problems Before They Start

The best way to protect your roofing investment is with regular, proactive maintenance. TPS Roofing Solutions offers maintenance programs for both residential and commercial properties — keeping your roof in peak condition and your warranty intact.

Minor Repair Maintenance

Small fixes handled before they become big problems. Cracked sealant, lifted shingles, minor flashing issues — we catch them early.

Roof Sealing & Coating

Periodic re-sealing and coating application to maintain waterproofing and reflective performance over the life of your roof.

Roof Inspections & Tune-Ups

Scheduled inspections with detailed reporting. We check every component, document conditions, and recommend proactive action.

Debris Removal

Clearing accumulated debris from roof surfaces, valleys, and drainage systems. Especially important for commercial flat roofs.
